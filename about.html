About Me

Hi, I'm Vlad, mostly known online as DalvSync. I am a 16-year-old developer based in Dnipro, Ukraine.

Beyond games, I still enjoy diving into the lower levels of systems, writing in C and Python, and daily-driving Arch Linux.
